Section 1: comparing categories

Homepage

Data at a glance

Here we have the top 10 most widespread tree types for all the cities in the USA.
With the dropdown button, it is possible to choose a single city of the ones in the dataset and visualize the same information but localized.

City comparison

Here we have three different types of stacked bar charts to show, in the state of California, the tree distribution for each city.
The stacks represent the 5 most widespread tree types (with the 6th being all the other tree types together) in California as a whole.
With the dropdown button, it is possible to cut the visualization to a selected number of cities, ordered by total number of trees.
The exception is the "stacked bar chart 100%", where the cities are ordered by the number of trees of the first and most widespread type.

City comparison - heatmap

Here we visualize the heatmap of the Californian subset.
We have the 15 cities of the state on the x-axis and the 15 most widespread tree types with respect to the state on the y-axis.
As it is shown also in the legend, the more intense the color on the heatmap, the more trees of that type are present in that city.
