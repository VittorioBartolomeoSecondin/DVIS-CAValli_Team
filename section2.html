Assignment 2: distributions

Sankey Diagram of the tree families in the USA

This is the visualization of the sankey diagram with the full dataset.
The ramification start by splitting the states in the north from the states in the south, then goes on to split the most important states in their major cities and, at last, for each of those, multiple links are drawn to represent the quantity of trees belonging to the 20 most widespread tree families.

For an easier visualization, you can click a node to highlight all the links that get to it.
